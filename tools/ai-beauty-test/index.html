How to Use AI Beauty Test

Using the AI Beauty Test is simple and fun! Follow these steps:

  • Select a Feature: Click on one of the buttons in the header navigation ("Scan & Score", "Look-Alike", "Face Parts", "Showdown") to choose the analysis you want to perform.
  • Upload Image(s): Click the "Upload your image" button within the selected feature section. Choose a clear photo of a face from your device. For "Beauty Showdown", you'll need to upload two images.
  • Wait for Analysis: The tool will simulate an AI analysis. You'll see a loading indicator with a percentage showing the progress. Please be patient, as the analysis time increases with each use for dramatic effect!
  • View Results: Once the analysis is complete, your results (scores, celebrity match, text analysis, or comparison) will be displayed automatically in the results area below the image preview.
  • Try Another!: Feel free to try different features or upload new images to see different simulated results. Remember, results for the same filename will be consistent within a session.

Note: This tool is for entertainment purposes only. The "AI" analysis is simulated and based on the filename for consistent demonstration.
