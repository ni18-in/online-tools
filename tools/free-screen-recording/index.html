How to Use

Follow these simple steps to record your screen:

  1. Navigate to Home: Ensure you are on the "Home" tab.
  2. Configure Settings (Optional):
    • Check "Record Audio" if you want to include audio from your microphone and/or system.
    • If "Record Audio" is checked, you can further specify if you want to record "System Audio" (audio playing from the selected tab/window) and/or "Microphone".
  3. Start Recording: Click the "Start Recording" button.
  4. Choose Screen: Your browser will prompt you to select what you want to share: your entire screen, a specific application window, or a browser tab. Choose your preference and click "Share".
  5. Recording Controls:
    • Once recording starts, you'll see a timer and a visual indicator.
    • Use "Pause" to temporarily halt the recording.
    • Use "Resume" to continue a paused recording.
    • Use "Stop Recording" to finish. You'll be asked for confirmation.
  6. Download: After stopping, your video will appear in the "Downloads" section. Click the filename to download the .webm video file.
  7. Permissions: The first time you use the app, your browser will ask for permission to access your screen and microphone. Please grant these permissions for the app to function correctly.

Note: All recordings are processed and stored locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.

About This App

This Screen Recorder is a lightweight, client-side web application that allows you to easily record your screen, specific application windows, or browser tabs directly from your web browser.

Key Features:

  • Client-Side Recording: All processing happens in your browser. No data is sent to any server, ensuring your privacy.
  • Multiple Recording Options: Choose to record your entire screen, a single window, or a browser tab.
  • Audio Recording: Optionally include audio from your microphone and/or system audio (from the shared tab/window).
  • Simple Controls: Easy-to-use start, pause, resume, and stop functionality.
  • Instant Download: Recordings are available for download as .webm files immediately after stopping.
  • Material Design: A clean, modern, and responsive interface.
  • Dark Mode: Toggle between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing.

Use Cases:

  • Creating software tutorials or demonstrations.
  • Recording gameplay.
  • Capturing presentations or online meetings (ensure you have consent).
  • Bug reporting by visually showing an issue.
  • Saving online videos or streams (respect copyright and terms of service).

This application utilizes the browser's built-in MediaStream Recording API, making it efficient and secure.
