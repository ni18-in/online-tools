About This App

The "Next Gen GST Calculator" is a user-friendly tool designed to help citizens and businesses navigate the new Goods and Services Tax (GST) reforms. Based on the proposed two-slab structure, this application provides a simple way to calculate the total price of an item including the new tax rates. It is a zero-service-cost tool, providing essential financial information transparently and easily.

How to Use

  1. Choose a product category from the cards above, such as "Daily Essentials" or "Automobiles."
  2. Select the specific item you want to calculate from the dropdown menu. This will automatically update the GST rate in the calculator section.
  3. Alternatively, if you already know the rate, you can manually select it from the "Select GST Rate" dropdown.
  4. Enter the base price of the item in the "Enter Base Price" field.
  5. Click the "Calculate GST" button to see the total price, including the GST amount.

Advantages of the App

  • Simplicity and Accuracy: The app automates complex GST calculations, reducing the risk of human error and saving you valuable time.
  • Instant Results: Get the total price and GST amount instantly without manual effort.
  • Clarity and Transparency: By showing the base price, GST rate, and final price, the tool provides a clear and transparent view of your tax obligations.
  • No Cost: This is a free-to-use web application with no hidden charges or service costs.
